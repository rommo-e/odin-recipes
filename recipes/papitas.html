Crunchy fries

Spicy n touch

1 hour away of hapiness, these fries makes me so happy that after a workout or a very long day I have energy to use , just to taste these fries

What you will need

  • Potatoes
  • Cayenna
  • Salt
  • Ice
  • Olive Oil
After you have all the ingredients is time to follow the ritual
  1. First wash the potatoes with only water make sure it doesn't have any sign of brotes
  2. Peel or don't the potatoes, then chop in large finger chips, afteer this cover with water and ice for 30 minutes /li>
  3. Dry completely using a towel
  4. Season with cayenna, olive, garlic and salt
  5. Let them marinate during 20 minutes and cover with a na[pkin
  6. Bake at 420 F during 30 min , within an interval of 15 min to change the side of the chips
